Key insight
Same word structure (4 words, 2 sites each) — same logical slicing.
Different physical spacing (5.0 vs 12.0 x-spacing, 3.0 vs 8.0 y-spacing).
Different internal connectivity (gate zone has buses, storage zone has none).
This is why zones own their own Grid — the coordinate system differs even though the logical slicing is identical.
