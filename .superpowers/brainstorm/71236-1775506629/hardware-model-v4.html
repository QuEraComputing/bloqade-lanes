Entangling zone pairs
entangling_zone_pairs: [(0, 1)]
Zones 0 and 1 are interleaved with a 2.0 shift — within blockade radius. Every (word_id, site_id) in zone 0 pairs 1:1 with the same address in zone 1.
Non-entangling pairs
Zones 2 and 3 are interleaved with a 4.0 shift — too far for CZ. Atoms must be transported to the gate region (via zone buses) for entangling operations.
Key insight: zones come in interleaved pairs
The physical hardware naturally organizes into pairs of zones — one grid shifted relative to the other. Whether the shift is small enough for CZ gates determines if they form an entangling pair. All four zones share the same word/site structure (3 words, 2 sites each) — only the grid coordinates and bus connectivity differ.
